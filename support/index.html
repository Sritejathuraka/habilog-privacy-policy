Thank you for using Habilog. This page provides support information, FAQs, troubleshooting steps, and contact details.

About Habilog

Habilog is a simple and powerful habit tracking app designed to help you build better habits, stay consistent, and improve your daily life.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a habit?

Tap the plus button on the home screen, enter your habit name, category, schedule, and reminder if needed.

2. How do I edit or delete a habit?

Open the habit details screen and choose edit or delete options.

3. Does Habilog store my data online?

No. All your data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded or shared.

4. Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your iPhone Settings under Habilog.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app if habits or stats do not update.
  • Check iOS permissions for notifications.
  • Ensure your device is updated to the latest iOS version.
  • If the app crashes, reinstalling usually fixes the issue.

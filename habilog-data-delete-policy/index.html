Users of the HabiLog app can request deletion of their account and associated data by following the steps below:

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: habilog.customerservice@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: HabiLog Account Deletion
  3. Include your registered email address in the message

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Habit records and streak data
  • Associated Firestore documents

Data Retention

All requested data will be permanently deleted within 7 to 10 business days.

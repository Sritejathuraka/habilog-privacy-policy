Privacy Policy for Habilog

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Habilog ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Habilog mobile application.

Information We Collect

Habilog collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

Personal Information

  • Name: First name and last name
  • Email Address: For account identification
  • Date of Birth: Used for age-related features
  • Height: For health tracking purposes
  • Sex/Gender: For personalized experience

Habit Data

  • Habit Information: Habit names, categories, frequencies, reminders, and completion history
  • Custom Dates: Start and end dates for custom habit schedules
  • Notification Preferences: Reminder times and settings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  1. Provide Core Functionality: Display your habits, track progress, and send reminders
  2. Personalize Experience: Show appropriate greetings and tailor the interface
  3. Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your device using SharedPreferences

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device only
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not transmit, upload, or store your data on any external servers
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties
  • No Analytics: We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising services

Permissions

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you habit reminders at your scheduled times
  • Control: You can enable or disable notifications in your device settings at any time

Timezone Access

  • Purpose: To schedule notifications accurately based on your local time
  • Control: This is handled automatically by your device

Your Rights

  • Access: All data is accessible within the app
  • Modification: You can edit or update your information at any time through the app settings
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your local data
  • Export: Currently, data export is not available, but your data remains on your device

Children's Privacy

Habilog does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app or provide any personal information.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall Habilog, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Use

Habilog is available for download in multiple countries via the App Store. All privacy practices described in this policy apply regardless of your location. Your data remains stored locally on your device and is subject to your local data protection laws.

No Data Collection Statement

Important: Habilog does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data outside of your device. We have no access to your information, cannot retrieve it, and cannot provide support for data recovery if the app is uninstalled.
